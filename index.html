Processes That We Follow

View how we work on our landing page projects below.

Research

Prior to any project, we make sure to understand what you are offering, who your target audience is and what your main website goals are.

Planning

Planning would involve creating a preliminary content structure, wireframe ideation, etc. Essentially we plan out the best possible end-user flow that will optimize conversions for your offer.

UI/UX Design

Our typical landing page project involves design and development. Working on the design process involves working on a web brand kit. Then we work on low-fidelity and high-fidelity wireframes. Finally we move towards working on the final page designs.

Development

Once the landing page design is finalized, we begin working on development. At this stage, we typically finalize the content that will be implemented on the website.
